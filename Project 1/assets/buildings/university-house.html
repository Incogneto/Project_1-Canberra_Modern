University House

1954

University House is the first graduate accommodation on the ANU campus and is now part of the University and Graduate Houses community. We have over 60 graduate students from 20+ countries who form a thriving and supportive community of scholars pursuing postgraduate research or coursework degrees.

University House provides a quiet environment in which residents are encouraged to participate in the academic and social life of a graduate community, but also able to live and work independently and without being disturbed by others.
